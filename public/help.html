How to use our site

The Dashboard

In the dashboard, you can view your three most recently used functions. To see more information, edit, or use the functions, click on the button next to the formula. To see all of your functions, click the button. This will take you to a landing, where you can search your functions, sort by tag, and more.

View/Edit Menu

From this menu, you can view, use, and edit your function.

Viewing All Functions

When viewing all of your functions, they will automatically be sorted in alphabetical order. To view tagged functions, select the desired tag from the side menu.
